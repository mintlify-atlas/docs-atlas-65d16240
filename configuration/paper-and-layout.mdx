Paper size, orientation, and language direction can be set globally in [Global settings](/configuration/global-settings) and overridden on a per-feed basis in each [PDF feed](/fluent-forms/pdf-feeds).

## Orientation

| Value | Label | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| `P` | Portrait | Taller than wide (default) |
| `L` | Landscape | Wider than tall |

Portrait is appropriate for most form submissions, invoices, and letters. Use Landscape for wide tables or certificates.

## Language direction

| Value | Label | Use case |
|-------|-------|---------|
| `ltr` | LTR | Left-to-right scripts (Latin, Cyrillic, Greek, etc.) |
| `rtl` | RTL | Right-to-left scripts (Arabic, Hebrew, Syriac, etc.) |

When **RTL** is selected, mPDF reverses the text flow for the entire PDF. Use this setting alongside a compatible font from the [Arabic font group](/configuration/fonts) when rendering Arabic or Hebrew content.

<Tip>
  If your form collects content in multiple directions (for example, a bilingual form with both English and Arabic fields), configure language direction at the per-feed level rather than globally.
</Tip>

## Per-feed overrides

Paper size, orientation, and language direction set in [Global settings](/configuration/global-settings) apply to all forms by default. Individual PDF feeds let you override each of these values for a specific form.

To override on a per-feed basis:

1. Go to **Fluent Forms â†’ your form â†’ PDF Feeds**.
2. Open or create a feed.
3. Adjust **Paper size**, **Orientation**, or **Language direction** under the feed's settings tab.

Values left blank in a feed fall back to the global defaults.
